`HagiCode - Sponsor Pack` is closer to a supporter rewards pack than a feature unlock pack. Its value is not about pushing a number higher. It is about turning your support into something visible, durable, and easy to feel over time.

## Why it is worth buying

Sponsor Pack is for users who already understand where HagiCode is heading and want to support that direction directly through a DLC purchase.

It is not meant to expand proposal concurrency, and it is not meant to add more avatar content. Instead, it makes "supporting the project" a formal, clear, and personally visible purchase option. For many long-term users, that support is already part of the value.

## What supporters get

The rewards that can be stated reliably in the current documentation are:

- One exclusive dark theme
- One exclusive light theme
- One exclusive Steam achievement

The point of these rewards is not feature stacking. It is to make support produce a clear response. You keep seeing these exclusive items during long-term use, and that makes the relationship between you and the project feel less like a one-time purchase and more like ongoing participation.

## Who should buy it

- Users who want to support HagiCode's long-term development directly.
- Users who care about exclusive themes, exclusive achievements, and the identity of being a supporter.
- Users who already get their main functional needs from the base edition and want a more explicit way to show support.

If you believe in HagiCode's direction, want the project to keep moving, and also want that support to leave a visible mark on your account, Sponsor Pack is a very natural purchase.

If your main goal is concurrency expansion and interface enhancement, read [Turbo Engine DLC](../turbo-engine-dlc/) first. If you only want more free cosmetic content, read [All Beauties Pack](../all-beauties-pack/) first.

If you want to start with free content, continue with [All Beauties Pack](../all-beauties-pack/). If you care more about workflow efficiency during heavy daily use, jump straight to [Turbo Engine DLC](../turbo-engine-dlc/).
