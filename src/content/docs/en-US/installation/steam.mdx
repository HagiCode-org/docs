This page explains how to buy, install, and launch HagiCode through Steam. If you already use Steam and want the most familiar way to get started, this is usually the most direct path.

:::tip[Best for these users]
The Steam installation path fits these scenarios:

- You already have a working Steam account
- You want Steam to handle purchase, download, installation, and updates
- You prefer managing software from your Steam library
- You do not want to handle extra installers or version files manually
:::

## What Is the Steam Edition of HagiCode

The Steam edition of HagiCode is the desktop version distributed through the Steam store. It is suitable for users who want to complete purchase and installation directly from Steam.

Compared with other installation paths, the Steam edition focuses more on:

- **Centralized purchase flow**: buy directly from the Steam store page
- **Unified install entry**: install from your Steam library after purchase
- **Unified updates**: keep receiving updates through Steam
- **General-user friendly setup**: no need to learn extra deployment paths or package sources first

If you prefer a standard graphical local install, see [Desktop Installation](/en-US/installation/desktop). If you need a team or server deployment path, see [Docker Compose Installation](/en-US/installation/docker-compose).

## Before You Start

Before you buy and install HagiCode, confirm the following:

1. Steam is installed and can open normally
2. You are signed in to a Steam account that can complete payments and downloads
3. Your device has a stable network connection
4. You have enough free disk space for installation

:::note[Network and region note]
If the store page loads slowly, payment fails, or download speed is poor, first check your network environment, Steam store region, download region, and local firewall settings.
:::

## Buy HagiCode on Steam

1. Open the [HagiCode Steam store page](https://store.steampowered.com/app/4625540/Hagicode/)
2. Sign in to your Steam account
3. Review the product information, pricing, and available content
4. Add HagiCode to your cart or buy it directly
5. Complete checkout through the Steam payment flow

After the purchase is complete, HagiCode is added to your Steam library automatically.

:::tip[What you should see after purchase]
After a successful purchase, you will usually see HagiCode in:

- Your Steam library
- Your Steam purchase history
- The Steam store page with an indicator such as "In Library"
:::

## Install HagiCode

After purchase, you can install HagiCode directly from the Steam client.

1. Open the Steam client
2. Go to your library
3. Find **HagiCode**
4. Click **Install**
5. Choose an installation path
6. Wait for Steam to finish downloading and installing

When installation is complete, HagiCode in your Steam library will appear as ready to launch.

:::note[About the installation path]
If you have multiple Steam library folders, choose one with enough free space and normal read/write access. This usually makes later updates and file verification more reliable.
:::

## Launch HagiCode for the First Time

1. Select **HagiCode** in your Steam library
2. Click **Play** or **Launch**
3. Wait for the program to finish its first startup
4. Follow the on-screen prompts to enter the initialization flow

After the first launch, it is recommended to finish the initial setup wizard before creating projects or sessions.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### The Steam Store Page Does Not Open

If you cannot open the HagiCode Steam store page normally:

1. Confirm that both Steam and your browser can access the network
2. Check your current network, proxy, and firewall settings
3. Try opening the [HagiCode Steam store page](https://store.steampowered.com/app/4625540/Hagicode/) directly
4. If it still fails, try again later or switch to a different network environment

### I Bought It but Cannot Find HagiCode in My Library

If HagiCode does not appear in your library after purchase:

1. Make sure you are signed in to the Steam account that completed the purchase
2. Refresh the library page in the Steam client
3. Check your purchase history and confirm the order completed successfully
4. Restart the Steam client and check again

### The Download Is Slow or Installation Takes Too Long

If the download or installation is much slower than expected:

1. Check whether other programs are using most of your bandwidth
2. Confirm that your Steam download region is appropriate
3. Pause and resume the download
4. Make sure the target disk has enough free space and normal read/write access

### The App Does Not Launch After Installation

If Steam shows HagiCode as installed but it does not launch:

1. Restart the Steam client first
2. Try running Steam file integrity verification
3. Check whether your operating system is blocking the application
4. Confirm that local security software is not incorrectly blocking related processes
