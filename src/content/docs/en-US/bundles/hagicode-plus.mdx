Hagicode Plus is a value bundle for committed HagiCode users.

If you already know you want more than a first look and would rather step straight into a fuller, smoother, and more serious workflow setup, this is the more direct purchase path.

Buy it, and you unlock the more complete HagiCode productivity experience in one step.

The included [Turbo Engine DLC](../../dlc/turbo-engine-dlc/) lets you:

1. Raise your proposal concurrency cap from 3 to 32.
2. Unlock expanded documentation themes.
3. Customize the `Co-Authored-By` used by AI Compose Commit.
4. Enable interface customization such as custom avatars, logos, and brand titles.

That is the real appeal of Hagicode Plus: instead of starting with the base edition and circling back later for the most noticeable upgrade, you get the key productivity layer upfront.
